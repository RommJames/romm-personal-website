My Story

My story is not about perfection — it's about progress, persistence, and rising every time I fall. I started from the bottom as an average student, but over time I worked my way up, eventually earning awards and recognition in academics, skills and competitions, where I won bronze and gold medals in Web Technology at the district, regional, and national levels.

But I didn't stop there. I became a teacher at MFI Polytechnic Institute, helping others achieve what I had achieved. I coached students to gold medals, taught programming and web development, and learned that teaching is one of the best ways to grow.

Beyond tech, I'm a basketball player, a man of faith, and someone who believes that greatness isn't based on talent alone, but on dedication and belief in purpose.
